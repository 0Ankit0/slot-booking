# Business Rules - Slot Booking System

## Booking Rules
- A slot can only be booked once per time window.
- Reservations expire after 10 minutes if payment is not completed.

## Cancellation Rules
- Free cancellations allowed up to 24 hours before the slot.
- Late cancellations incur a fee (configurable per provider).

## Payment Rules
- Payment capture occurs on booking confirmation.
- Refunds are processed within 5 business days.

## No-Show Policy
- No-show penalties apply after two incidents within 30 days.
